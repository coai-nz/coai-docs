# Coai Roadmap

Coai is being built in stages, starting with the operational loop where workflow loss is immediate and visible, then expanding upward with discipline.

## Current phase

### Coai Field ↔ Site Console
Current beta focus is the operating loop between:

- **Coai Field** for frontline capture
- **Site Console** for single-project review, coordination, and project truth

Focus in this phase includes:

- tighter capture-to-review flow
- cleaner follow-through and response paths
- clearer project-bound truth
- stronger communication and record structure
- practical workflow closure under real site conditions

## Next expansion layers

### Main OS
As the ground-level loop strengthens, Coai expands upward into:

- cross-project routing
- department-facing governance
- wider operational coordination
- structured escalation and continuity

### Platform Console
Structural control layer for:

- identity
- permissions
- continuity
- diagnostics
- dangerous operations

### Vault
Controlled document and record layer for:

- drawings
- specifications
- revisions
- permits
- governed project records
- document truth and continuity

### Drawing-aware assistance
Future-facing support for:

- drawing reading
- document-aware interpretation
- practical project assistance with authority boundaries intact

### Executive Console
A later-phase oversight layer, deliberately staged rather than implied too early.

## Build philosophy

Coai is being built carefully, not rushed to look complete.

The system starts where operational pain is real, then earns its upper layers through stronger workflow closure, authority discipline, and clearer project truth.
